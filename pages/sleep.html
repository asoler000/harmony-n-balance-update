Sleep & Sound

Sleep begins with the environment around it. From portable sound machines with nature sounds and white noise to compact bedside designs and soft-glow sleep essentials, this collection is created to bring calm, comfort, and quiet beauty into the end of the day.

Explore the Collection
The Collection

Objects for quieter evenings.

Each piece in this collection is designed around a single intention — to make the end of the day feel more considered, more calm, and more restorative.

Sound Machine

Drift White Noise Station

Ten curated soundscapes — white noise, pink noise, and layered nature sounds — in a compact bedside form designed to feel as considered as the space around it.

Sleep Ambience

Evening Wind-Down Diffuser

Ultrasonic diffusion meets ambient warmth — a quiet presence that fills the room with the scent of letting go, thirty minutes before rest begins.

Ambient Light

Soft Glow Sleep Lamp

Sunset-simulating warmth that dims gradually through the evening. No harsh transitions. Just the kind of glow that signals the body to ease into rest.

A closer look

A softer light for the end of the day.

The glow machine was designed for the quieter hours — when brightness feels intrusive and the smallest details matter most. With a gentle illuminated presence and a compact bedside form, it brings warmth, atmosphere, and a sense of ease into the places where rest begins.

Created to feel beautiful on a nightstand, simple to live with, and calming to return to each evening.

Why the environment matters

Better rest starts with what surrounds you.

Harmony & Balance Sleep & Sound was built around one insight: the quality of your rest is shaped by the hour before it. These objects don't just help you sleep — they create the conditions where rest becomes easier to return to.

  • Layered soundscapes — white noise, pink noise, nature
  • Auto-off timer — 30, 60, or 90-minute increments
  • Compact bedside form — under 5 inches, plug-in or portable
  • Zero screen glare — designed to live beside the bed, not light it
