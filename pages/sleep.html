Sleep & Sound

The hour before sleep is the most underrated part of your day. These devices make it count — quieting the noise, softening the light, and giving your nervous system permission to finally let go.

Explore the Collection
Sleep & Sound

Designed for the quieter hours.

The Sleep & Sound collection brings together sound machines, ambient glow devices, and sleep-support tools — each one built to help create an environment where rest arrives more naturally. Compact, considered, and built for the bedside.

The thinking behind the collection

Better rest starts with what surrounds you.

Harmony & Balance Sleep & Sound products are built around one insight: the quality of your rest is shaped by the environment you create before it. These are not gadgets — they are quiet, functional objects that help make the transition to rest feel easier, softer, and more natural.

Whether it is the steady rhythm of white noise filling a room, or the gentle presence of a low-lit device on a nightstand, each piece in the collection is designed to reduce friction and support a calmer close to the day.

Quiet by design. Restful by intention.

What we offer

Within the collection.

  • 01 Portable Sound Machines Compact sound companions for white-noise and nature sounds — designed for the bedside, travel, or any space that needs a quieter atmosphere.
  • 02 Bedside Glow Devices Softly illuminated pieces for visual calm — low light that signals the close of the day without the disruption of screens or sharp light sources.
  • 03 Sleep Atmosphere Essentials Objects supporting a quieter environment through sound, light, and ease — the small, considered details that help the room feel more like rest.
